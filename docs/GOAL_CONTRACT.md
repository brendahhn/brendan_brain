# Goal Contract — Brendan OS

## The goal in one paragraph
One increasingly intelligent personal operating system: Personal OS (the website) is the
everyday cockpit; Brendan Brain (this repo) is the durable memory and coordination layer;
Claude Code is the operator/engineer; specialist robots are departments; everything visible
in ONE morning digest Brendan reads outside Claude. It gets smarter from his feedback and
keeps operating after Fable's access ends 2026-07-18.

## Success criteria (Brendan's words, distilled)
1. He opens Personal OS in the morning and sees a comprehensive tailored newspaper —
   health summary, idea-queue breakdowns, robot findings, tea business state, questions
   for him — without opening Claude ("I just don't like it being in Claude").
2. Anything he jots in Notes/Todos is scrubbed by the next daily run and lands in the
   right place ("massive file cabinet it autonomously uses").
3. The system notices things unasked (stale projects, trends, low stock) and SUGGESTS —
   "that you're able to suggest things I'm not even thinking of, that's big."
4. Nothing runs more than once a day; nothing interrupts; urgent = manual session.
5. It never loses his data, never sends/purchases/trades, never fabricates links/prices.
6. His AI setup never stagnates (standing growth mandate, D54).

## Cadence ceiling (hard)
Max one scheduled run per routine per day. All output channels through the daily paper.

## Non-goals
Real-money trading (paper forever; recommendations-only pathway at most, D26).
Employer/work data. Banking. Universal conversation capture (platform can't).
