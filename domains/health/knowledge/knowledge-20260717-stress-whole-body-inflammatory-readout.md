Stress is a whole-body inflammatory lever, not a single-organ problem. The same stress hormone
(CRH) that destabilizes the gut lining also triggers skin mast cells and weakens the skin
barrier, so one set of stress-management levers (breathing, sleep, cardio, acceptance/values
work) pays off across multiple organs at once — one lever, several outputs. A second principle
the robot attached from the same chapter: some medications turn an organ into something a
doctor must watch (e.g. periodic dermatologist skin-cancer exams on certain IBD drugs), so
routine screening can be medicine, not vanity. Flagged by health-robot (Run 24, skin chapter).
