The appearance levers that actually work are the ones already worth doing for health: good
posture (the single most evidence-based, free "appearance" lever, and it doubles as neck-health
and mobility), nasal breathing (airway/sleep), fragrance-free products (fewer allergens), and
regular scalp washing (hygiene). Meanwhile the viral "looksmaxxing" staples are weak-to-debunked
for adults — "mewing" does not remodel adult facial bone, gum-chewing does not change jaw-muscle
size, mouth-taping is thin plus a real safety risk. So appearance rides free on the health stack
rather than needing its own supplements or gadgets. Flagged by health-robot (Run 25, appearance
chapter). Not yet Brendan-confirmed.
