The mouth is upstream of the gut and heart, not a separate system. Oral bacteria are swallowed
constantly and, when the wrong ones colonize the gut, can drive the same inflammation that
underlies bowel disease; gum disease also tracks with ~20% higher cardiovascular risk, and
regular flossing associates with lower stroke and atrial-fibrillation rates. So daily
mechanical cleaning (floss / interdental brush / water flosser) is a genuine, near-free health
lever, not cosmetic. Two counter-intuitive corollaries the robot attached: a daily
antibacterial mouthwash can nudge blood pressure UP (it kills nitrate-reducing tongue bacteria),
and it is worth checking labels for titanium dioxide (E171, banned from EU food in 2022) in
whitening toothpaste. Flagged by health-robot (Run 23, oral-health chapter). Not yet
Brendan-confirmed.
