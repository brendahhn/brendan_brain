Erectile function is a free cardiovascular early-warning system: erectile dysfunction
independently predicts heart attacks/strokes and tends to appear ~3-5 years before a cardiac
event (smaller penile arteries clog symptomatically first). The upshot is that there is no
separate protocol — the same things that protect the heart (blood pressure, ApoB/lipids,
fitness, not smoking) protect sexual function. Instance of a broader pattern worth carrying
cross-domain: a visible symptom can be an early readout of an upstream system. Flagged by
health-robot (Run 22, reproductive-health chapter). Not yet Brendan-confirmed.
