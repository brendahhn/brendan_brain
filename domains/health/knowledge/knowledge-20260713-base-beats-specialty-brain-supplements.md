Most durable brain-health wins come from the ordinary base — lifelong cardiorespiratory
fitness, protected deep sleep, midlife blood-pressure and LDL/ApoB control, not smoking,
treating hearing loss, and building cognitive reserve — not from specialty supplements
(MIND diet, cocoa flavanols, urolithin-A, NMN, lion's mane were null or unproven). The
Lancet Commission places ~45% of dementia risk in the potentially-modifiable column.
Flagged by health-robot (Run 19) as a cross-domain principle. Not yet Brendan-confirmed.
